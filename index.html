Withstanding every challenge with courage

Efficient testing techniques make our systems further reliable and foolproof. We utilise modelling software to simulate certain wind conditions to examine how aerodynamics will work for a particular UAV. UAVs then undergo certain strict testing parameters for every electronic, mechanical and aerodynamic Al change.

We have tested our UAVs successfully in the rough conditions of Pokhran, high altitudes of Ladakh, high wind pressure of Gulmarg, rainy and foggy terrain of North East and coastal areas like Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

KNOWLEDGE BECOMES POWER WHEN APPLY

We use absolute precision and unique capabilities to develop and deliver tailor-made solutions for every requirement. The application of our UAVs ranges from facilitating agriculture activities to carrying out logistics maintenance and surveillance for our forces.

COMPREHENSIVE TRAINING AND LIFELONG SUPPORT

We impart comprehensive two-month training to the personnel in our testing facility. The training includes initial hands-on simulation, field training for UAV readiness and flights, environmental awareness for safe UAV operations, maintenance and life cycle of the UAV components, and autonomous operation from the ground control station.

We also provide 24×7 on-site training and operational support to the users, assisting them to operate their UAV setup successfully.
